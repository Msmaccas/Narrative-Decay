# Pricing

NarrativeDecay is envisioned as a professional tool for discretionary traders, research boutiques and portfolio managers who value time‑savings and transparency.  Below is a proposed pricing structure for the production version.  The prototype in this repository is free and for demonstration only.

| Plan | Price (USD / month) | Suitable for | Features |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Starter** | $49 | Individual traders and hobbyists | Access to up to 10 named theses; hourly narrative updates; web dashboard; API access with rate limits; e‑mail notifications for decaying narratives. |
| **Professional** | $199 | Active portfolio managers and small funds | Up to 50 theses; 15‑minute updates; custom half‑life settings; Slack/Teams integrations; priority support; ability to export CSV/JSON ledger. |
| **Enterprise** | Custom | Research boutiques and hedge funds | Unlimited theses; custom provider integrations (news, filings, transcripts); on‑premise deployment or VPC; dedicated support; SSO; compliance features and audit trails.  Pricing negotiated based on usage and support level. |

### Add‑ons

* **Additional data providers** – priced per provider depending on API costs.  For example, adding a premium news feed or real‑time filings ingestion would incur additional fees.
* **Custom agent development** – bespoke agent roles (e.g., sector specialist or macroeconomist) can be developed at an hourly rate.

### Payment terms

All plans are billed monthly with a discount for annual commitments.  A free trial may be offered for the Starter tier.  VAT/GST and other taxes are charged where applicable.  Payments are processed securely via a third‑party provider.

### Disclaimer

Pricing is indicative and subject to change prior to launch.  NarrativeDecay is a research tool and does not provide financial advice.
